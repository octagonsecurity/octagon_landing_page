Surface container for services, features, and reviews — optional top photo, title, body, footer.

```jsx
<Card
  image="assets/svc-cameras.png"
  title="Security Cameras & Surveillance"
  accent
  interactive
  footer={<Button variant="ghost">Learn More</Button>}
>
  High-quality camera systems with clear video and remote access.
</Card>
```

Props: `image`/`imageAlt`, `title`, `accent` (red top border), `interactive` (hover lift), `footer`.
