Labelled text field for contact / quote forms. Uppercase label, red focus ring.

```jsx
<Input label="Name" placeholder="Jane Doe" required />
<Input label="Email" type="email" hint="We'll reply within one business day." />
<Input label="Message" multiline placeholder="Tell us about your property…" />
```

Props: `label`, `required`, `hint`, `invalid`, `multiline`. Forwards standard input/textarea attributes.
